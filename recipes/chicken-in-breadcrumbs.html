Chiken In Breadcrumbs

Description

In this recipe you will learn how to make chiken in breadcrumbs, a delicious food that I risk saying it's my favourite.

Ingredients

  • chiken stripes(or your preferent cut);
  • 1 egg;
  • breadcrumbs;
  • kitchen oil.

Steps

  1. crack an egg in a plate and mix it up;
  2. get a chicken strip and bathe it the mixed egg;
  3. roll the chicken strip in a plate full of breadcrumbs;
  4. fill the botton of a pan with kitchen oil;
  5. heat up the pan until you feel like the oil is ready for the chicken;
  6. put the chicken strip in the oil, you will know it's ready because it makes a lot of noise a spills oil, so take care;
  7. wait until the chicken gets the right color, and take it off the oil;
  8. let it rest for some seconds and voila, your your chicken in breadcrumbs ready to be eaten!!
