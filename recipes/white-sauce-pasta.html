White Sauce Pasta

Description

In this recipe you will learn how to make white sauce pasta.

Ingredients

  • pasta of your choice;
  • 1 cup of milk.
  • 1 spoon of corn starch.
  • 1 box of heavy cream.
  • salt.
  • onions.
  • 1 spoon of butter.

Steps

  1. fill a pan with water, enough water so that the pasta gets completely covered;
  2. heat up the pan until the water starts boiling, then add the pasta and some salt;
  3. get another pan to make the sauce;
  4. put 1 spoon of butter and some small pieces of onion;
  5. put the heavy cream when the onions start to get golden;
  6. put the corn starch spoon in the cup of milk and mix it;
  7. put the mix in the pan when the heavy cream starts to boil;
  8. keep mixing it until it all starts boiling, then turn the fire off and let it rest;
  9. have a look at the pasta to see if it is ready, then take the water off;
  10. mix the pasta with the sauce and you are ready to go;
