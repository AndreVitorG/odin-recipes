Instant Noodles

Description

In this recipe you will learn how to make instant noodles, a simple and easy dish for a busy or lazy person.

Ingredients

  • raw noodles;
  • flavor powder.

Steps

  1. fill a pan with water, enough water so that the noodles get completely covered;
  2. heat up the pan until the water starts boiling, then add the noodles;
  3. keep it in the boiling water until it gets loose and less yellowish;
  4. turn the fire off and take most of the water out of the recipient;
  5. mix the flavor powder with the noodles and voila, your instant noodles are ready to be eaten!!
